## Module Role

`rf2o_laser_odometry` is the 2D laser odometry option used when a lighter-weight scan-based odometry chain is enough.

## Typical Use

- 2D localization support
- simple testbeds
- lightweight odometry chains where full 3D LIO is unnecessary
